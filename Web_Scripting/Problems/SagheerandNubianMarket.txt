On his trip to Luxor and Aswan Sagheer went to a Nubian market to buy some souvenirs for his friends and relatives The market has some strange rules It contains n different items numbered from 1 to n The ith item has base cost ai Egyptian pounds If Sagheer buys k items with indices x1x2xk then the cost of item xj is axjxjk for 1jk In other words the cost of an item is equal to its base cost in addition to its index multiplied by the factor kSagheer wants to buy as many souvenirs as possible without paying more than S Egyptian pounds Note that he cannot buy a souvenir more than once If there are many ways to maximize the number of souvenirs he will choose the way that will minimize the total cost Can you help him with this task
