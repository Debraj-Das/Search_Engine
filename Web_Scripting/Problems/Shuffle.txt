You are given a binary string ie a string consisting of characters 0 andor 1 s of length n You can perform the following operation with the string s at most once choose a substring a contiguous subsequence of s having exactly k characters 1 in it and shuffle it reorder the characters in the substring as you wishCalculate the number of different strings which can be obtained from s by performing this operation at most once
