Igor the analyst has adopted n little bunnies As we all know bunnies love carrots Thus Igor has bought a carrot to be shared between his bunnies Igor wants to treat all the bunnies equally and thus he wants to cut the carrot into n pieces of equal area Formally the carrot can be viewed as an isosceles triangle with base length equal to 1 and height equal to h Igor wants to make n1 cuts parallel to the base to cut the carrot into n pieces He wants to make sure that all n pieces have the same area Can you help Igor determine where to cut the carrot so that each piece have equal area  Illustration to the first example
