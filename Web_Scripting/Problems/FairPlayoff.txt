Four players participate in the playoff tournament The tournament is held according to the following scheme the first player will play with the second and the third player with the fourth then the winners of the pairs will play in the finals of the tournamentIt is known that in a match between two players the one whose skill is greater will win The skill of the ith player is equal to si and all skill levels are pairwise different ie there are no two identical values in the array sThe tournament is called fair if the two players with the highest skills meet in the finalsDetermine whether the given tournament is fair
