Sereja showed an interesting game to his friends The game goes like that Initially there is a table with an empty cup and n water mugs on it Then all players take turns to move During a move a player takes a nonempty mug of water and pours all water from it into the cup If the cup overfills then we assume that this player lostAs soon as Serejas friends heard of the game they wanted to play it Sereja on the other hand wanted to find out whether his friends can play the game in such a way that there are no losers You are given the volumes of all mugs and the cup Also you know that Sereja has n1 friends Determine if Serejas friends can play the game so that nobody loses
