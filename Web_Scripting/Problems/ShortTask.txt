Let us denote by d(n) the sum of all divisors of the number n, i.e. d(n) = \sum\limits_{k | n} k.For example, d(1) = 1, d(4) = 1+2+4=7, d(6) = 1+2+3+6=12.For a given number c, find the minimum n such that d(n) = c.
